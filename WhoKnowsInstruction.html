Who Knows ? Instructions

This game checks the knowledge that you learned in the

Learning Section and particularly in the Info about

Countries.

In this game you will have 4 response options and you

should choose the right based on the question that

appears on the screen and is associated with information

of Info about the Countries.
In this game there are questions for all countries, no

country select. The game consists 3 levels, where as you

go along levels, the difficulty of the questions increases.
In this game you have at your disposal two lives, ie the

game ends when you have totaled two wrong attempts

at any level of the game. On each wrong answer you

return at the beginning of the current level.

Points Calculation:

First level -> 200 Points

Second level -> 500 Points

Third level -> 1000 Points

Each life worths 100 Points
