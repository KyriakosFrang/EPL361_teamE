Spot the Country Instructions

For this game no knowledge of the previous part of the game

is needed.All you need to know is some necessary

knowledge in Geography.

This game consists of two smaller games. In one game you

are asked to match the name of any country or the sound of

its name that is in English with the flag of the country. In

the other game you can see the map of Europe where you

are asked to match the name of each country with the

country's position on the map.

These two games change randomly so you don't have to

complete one of the games in order to play the other but

you can play both games.

In this game too you have at your disposal three lives.

That is to say the game ends when you have totaled three

wrong attempts at any level of the game. The 'unpress'

button is to cancel your current choice and make a new one.

Points Calculation:

Correct answer: 10 Points

Incorrect answer: -10 Points

Each life worths 20 Points
