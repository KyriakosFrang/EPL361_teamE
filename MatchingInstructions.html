Matching Instructions

This game checks the knowledge that you learned in the

Learning Section and particularly in the Words with Sound.

In this game you can match the words you learned with

the default English word but first you have to choose the

language that you want to play. After you choose the

language you want to play, you can start playing.

The game consists 3 levels and each level has 2 stages

except from level 3 which has 3 stages. In the first level

you have 4 words from the language you have chosen

and 4 possible answers. In the second level you have 2

words and 4 possible answers to match. In the third and

final level you have one word and 4 possible answers.

In this game you have at your disposal three lives, ie the

game ends when you have totaled three wrong attempts

at any level of the game. The 'unpress' button is to cancel

your current choice and make a new one.

Points Calculation:

Correct answer: 10 Points

Incorrect answer: -10 Points

Each life worths 20 Points
